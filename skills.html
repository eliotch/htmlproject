One thing I can teach to others is how to fold a crane using paper
Steps:

  1. Grab a piece of paper
  2. Fold a triangle on one side
  3. Fold the excess paper, then rip it off so you have a square
  4. Fold in half, then unfold, then turn 90 degrees and fold in half again
  5. Fold triangles on opposite sides of the folded half
  6. Open up the paper to have a diamond shape
  7. Fold triangles on the left and right side of the diamond on both sides
  8. Close both sides by having the left triangle touch the right triangle
  9. lift the non triangle sides up
  10. Crease one of the triangle sides for a head shape
  11. Spread the wings to add width to your crane, and you are done
